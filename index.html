Rest Games is an independent studio rooted in the belief that games don't need to demand everything from you. We create worlds worth getting lost in — then finding your way back out.

Every title we release is crafted with care and intention. No roadmaps. No live service. Just finished, thoughtful games that respect your time.

Our Philosophy
"The best games know when to stop talking and just let you be."

We resist the compulsion loop. We ignore engagement metrics. We make games that have endings — real ones — and that feel complete when you put them down. We believe in craft over content, silence over noise, and that a single perfect moment in a game is worth more than a hundred forgettable hours.
